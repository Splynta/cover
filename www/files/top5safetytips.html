Top 5 Safety Tips from Greater Manchester Police Twitter Chat

Missed GMP’s twitter chat aimed at students to have their chance to ask any question about student safety?

Cover4students have taken away the top 5 safety tips from the twitter chat for students.

  • 1. Lock windows and doors – 1 in 3 burglaries because homes aren't left secure.
  • 2. Students with Smartphone and tablets should install tracking software and register valuables on www.immobilise.com
  • 3. Students can dramatically reduce their chances of being a victim of phone theft in public by keeping it away from view.
  • 4. Students can help each other by sharing crime prevention messages to keep each other safe.
  • 5. Try not to walk home alone at night, stay in groups and stick to well lit areas. It is also advisable to avoid shortcuts and use licensed taxis.
